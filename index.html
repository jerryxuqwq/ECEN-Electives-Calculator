TAMU ECEN Elective Requirement Calculator

This tool is intended to be used as a guide only. Schedule an appointment with an ECEN advisor to confirm that your planned courses are meeting the ECEN electives requirement.
To use this calculator:
  1. Select your catalog year, follow the step from Canvas to determine your catalog year
  2. Input your ecen course number, format "ECEN XXX"
  3. The requirement will be calculated automatically
  4. "Tab" and "Shift+Tab" can be used to jump between input boxes.
